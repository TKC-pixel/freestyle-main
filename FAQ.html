Frequently Asked Questions


  • What is the minimum age requirement for mentees?

    We welcome mentees from all age groups, from young students to working professionals, and our mentors are experienced in guiding and mentoring diverse mentees.

  • How are mentors and mentees matched?

    We use a sophisticated matching algorithm that takes into account various factors, including mentee’s goals, preferences, and learning style, and mentor’s expertise, availability, and personality. Our goal is to create a mutually beneficial and successful mentorship relationship.

  • How often do mentorship sessions take place?

    The frequency and duration of mentorship sessions depend on the mentee’s goals and needs, as well as the mentor’s availability and preferences. We encourage regular and consistent interactions between mentors and mentees to ensure progress and success.

  • What is the process for booking an online mentoring session?

    Booking an online tutoring session is easy and convenient. Simply log in to your account, select the mentor you want, and choose a suitable date and time. You can also communicate with your mentor in advance to discuss your specific needs and goals.

  • Can I connect with multiple mentors?

    Yes, you can connect with multiple mentors. Having a diverse network of mentors can provide different perspectives and insights, enriching your overall learning and development experience.
